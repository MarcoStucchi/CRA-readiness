# Annex VI – Simplified EU Declaration of Conformity

> **Regulation (EU) 2024/2847 – adopted 23 October 2024, in force 10 December 2024**

> **New annex — no equivalent in the 2022 proposal.** Introduced to enable a lightweight declaration that can be physically affixed to or accompany the product (e.g. on packaging or in a quick-start document), while the full EU declaration of conformity (Annex V) remains accessible online. Referenced in Article 13(20) and Article 28(2).

---

The simplified EU declaration of conformity referred to in Article 13(20) shall be drawn up as follows:

Hereby, [name of manufacturer] declares that the product with digital elements [name and type of product] is in conformity with Regulation (EU) 2024/2847 of the European Parliament and of the Council.

The full text of the EU declaration of conformity is available at the following internet address: [internet address]

> **Note on use:** The manufacturer may use this simplified form in place of the full Annex V declaration when affixing a declaration directly to the product or its packaging. The full Annex V declaration must nonetheless be drawn up, kept at the disposal of market surveillance authorities, and made available via the internet address referenced in the simplified declaration. Both declarations must be kept for at least 10 years after the product has been placed on the market, or for the support period, whichever is longer.
