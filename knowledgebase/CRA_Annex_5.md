# Annex V – EU Declaration of Conformity

> **Regulation (EU) 2024/2847 – adopted 23 October 2024, in force 10 December 2024**

> **Changes from the 2022 proposal:** Renumbered from Annex IV. Content updated to reflect the final article structure (references updated to Articles 28–30). A new companion **Annex VI** covers a simplified version of this declaration that may be affixed directly to the product, with the full declaration available online.

---

The EU declaration of conformity referred to in Article 28 shall contain all of the following information:

(1) Name and type and any additional information enabling the unique identification of the product with digital elements, including a photograph or illustration where appropriate;

(2) Name and address of the manufacturer or, where applicable, his authorised representative;

(3) A statement that the EU declaration of conformity is issued under the sole responsibility of the manufacturer;

(4) Object of the declaration: identification of the product with digital elements allowing traceability, including a photograph where appropriate;

(5) A statement that the object of the declaration described above is in conformity with the relevant Union harmonisation legislation, in particular with Regulation (EU) 2024/2847;

(6) References to any relevant harmonised standards used or references to any other common specifications or cybersecurity certification schemes under Regulation (EU) 2019/881 in relation to which conformity is declared;

(7) Where applicable, the name and identification number of the notified body, a description of the conformity assessment procedure performed, and identification of the certificate issued;

(8) The support period of the product with digital elements;

(9) Additional information:

Signed for and on behalf of: …………………………………

(place and date of issue):

(name, function) (signature):
